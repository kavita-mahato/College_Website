About UCET Hazaribagh

Excellence in Engineering Education Since 2010

Our Institution

University College of Engineering and Technology Hazaribagh (UCETH) is a premier autonomous institution established in 2010 by an Act of Parliament. We are committed to providing world-class engineering education and fostering innovation.

Our Mission

To develop competent engineers with strong technical skills, ethical values, and leadership qualities who can contribute to the nation's technological advancement and global competitiveness.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of higher learning, known for excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation, producing leaders who shape the future of technology.

"Welcome to UCET Hazaribagh, where innovation meets excellence!"

Greetings and warm welcome to all at University College of Engineering and Technology Hazaribagh, India. UCET Hazaribagh is one of the premier autonomous institutions of national importance under the aegis of the Ministry of Education, Government of India.

Our institution was established in 2010 with state-of-the-art infrastructure and world-class faculty. We are committed to providing quality education, fostering research and innovation, and developing globally competent engineers who contribute meaningfully to society.
